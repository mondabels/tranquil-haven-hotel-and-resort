Come and dine with us at Tranquil Haven's Restaurant and Bar!


Coastal Breeze Dining and Sands & Spirits Bar

Embark on a delectable journey through coastal flavors and seafood delights at Coastal Breeze Dining, where our buffet extravaganza awaits. The restaurant, boasting panoramic ocean views, sets the stage for an unforgettable dining experience.

Indulge in a feast for the senses with our diverse selection of fresh seafood and locally-inspired dishes. From succulent shrimp to mouthwatering crab legs, every bite is a celebration of the sea's bounty. The stylish and comfortable setting provides the perfect backdrop for enjoying your culinary discoveries.

After a delightful meal, head to Sands & Spirits Bar, a chic and inviting space where guests can savor handcrafted cocktails and fine spirits. Against the backdrop of the setting sun over the ocean, this stylish bar offers a laid-back ambiance ideal for socializing and toasting to the coastal lifestyle. Cheers to unforgettable moments by the sea!
